Session Attack project

Session attacks, often referred to as post-exploitation or in-session attacks, occur after an attacker has successfully gained access to a system. These attacks focus on maintaining control, escalating privileges, and exfiltrating sensitive data. Once an attacker is inside a system, they may perform various actions to exploit the compromised system further.

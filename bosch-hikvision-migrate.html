Project Summary

I led the migration from **Bosch analogue DVRs** to **Hikvision 32-/64-channel NVRs** across Mobil facilities and offshore vessels. The project introduced hybrid cloud storage, 90-day retention, and centralized remote viewing via iVMS. My role included system planning, NVR configuration, migration of camera streams, Cisco switch VLAN setup, redundant UPS integration, and staff training for monitoring and playback. The project significantly modernized Mobil’s surveillance infrastructure.

My responsibilities

  • Planned and led CCTV migration strategy (Bosch → Hikvision)
  • Configured NVRs with multi-SATA storage for extended retention
  • Enabled iVMS remote access for shore/offshore monitoring
  • Integrated UPS for CCTV resilience and disaster recovery
  • Configured Cisco switch VLANs for IP camera segmentation
  • Coordinated with site staff and trained operators post-migration

Technologies used

Bosch DVR Hikvision NVR Hikvision iVMS Cisco Switches Eaton UPS Windows Server 2012

Impact & Outcome

Before: Bosch DVRs limited retention to 30 days, with poor remote access and no redundancy.

After: Hikvision NVRs enabled 90-day storage, remote cloud/PC access, UPS protection, and centralized monitoring across Mobil vessels and seaports.

Business value: Improved compliance, reduced downtime, modernized IT security posture, and increased operational visibility.

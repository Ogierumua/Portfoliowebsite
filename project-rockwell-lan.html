Project Summary

I participated in the Rockwell Automation LAN/WiFi upgrade at their Milton Keynes site, representing Sentinel Technologies. My role focused on switch migration and LAN cutover. Tasks included removing old switches, racking new Cisco 9300 series devices, applying patch cables, supporting AP hanging and testing, and assisting with traffic migration under remote guidance via AnyDesk/MS Teams.

My Responsibilities

  • Rack and cable Cisco 9300 series switches
  • Assist ComTech team with AP installation (8 total)
  • Perform LAN cutover with console access & PuTTY
  • Upload site photos to WhatsApp group
  • Coordinate with Rockwell Lead Engineer remotely

Technologies Used

Cisco 9300 Series LAN/WiFi APs PuTTY AnyDesk MS Teams

Impact & Outcome

Before: The LAN/WiFi migration modernized the network infrastructure at Rockwell Automation’s Milton Keynes site, replacing outdated switching hardware with Cisco 9300 series, and ensuring improved reliability, scalability, and performance. Before: Legacy Cisco switches with limited support and inconsistent WiFi coverage.

After: Modernized LAN backbone, properly patched and documented, improved WiFi coverage, and validated connectivity with minimal operational disruption. This project demonstrated my ability to work on-site under tight schedules, coordinate with global teams, and deliver critical infrastructure upgrades efficiently

Business value: Modernized LAN and WiFi infrastructure with Cisco 9300 series—delivering improved reliability, scalability, and performance in just 2 weeks with minimal operational disruption..

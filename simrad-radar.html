Project Summary

I led the installation and integration of **Simrad radar systems** onboard Mobil vessels, delivering safe and compliant deployment of radar hardware, integration with vessel servers and navigation systems, and connectivity back to shore for monitoring. The work included rack mounting radar interfaces, integrating radar output with VMware-hosted servers and HMI/bridge consoles, configuring network routes/VLANs, ensuring power resilience (UPS), and setting up monitoring and alerting with PRTG.

My responsibilities

  • Lead installation and commissioning of Simrad radar units and interfaces
  • Server integration: VMware ESXi host provisioning and iLO hardware management
  • Network: VLAN and routing config on Cisco switches; firewall rules for telemetry
  • PRTG monitoring setup for radar, comms, and server health
  • Integration of radar data with bridge display systems and shop-floor monitoring
  • Onsite coordination with vessel crew and shipyard for secure mounting and cabling

Technologies used

Simrad Radar VMware ESXi iLO PRTG Cisco Switches Eaton UPS Teams / Ship comms

Impact & Outcome

Before: Radar hardware was not fully integrated with the vessel IT stack; monitoring and remote diagnostics were limited.

After: Radar feeds integrated with onboard servers and bridge displays, remote monitoring via PRTG and shore-side support enabled, improved redundancy via UPS and redundant comms — increasing safety and reducing diagnosis time for radar-related incidents.

Business value: Increased navigational awareness, faster troubleshooting, and better compliance with operational safety standards.

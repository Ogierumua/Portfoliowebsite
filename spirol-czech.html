Project Summary

I was assigned to commission Spirol’s new manufacturing and warehouse facility in Prague, Czech Republic. The scope included Juniper network deployment, CATO SD-WAN integration, Teams telephony rollout, Vodafone circuit activation, and Genetec security compliance. My role involved coordinating with US, UK, and EU IT leaders and external vendors to ensure the site was fully operational before warehouse relocation.

My responsibilities

  • Deployment and configuration of Juniper switches and access points
  • Setup and testing of CATO SD-WAN appliance for WAN integration
  • Microsoft Teams telephony configuration with Pure IP
  • Ensuring security compliance (Genetec camera platform)
  • Coordinating Vodafone internet circuit installation
  • Multi-phase visits (June–August 2024) for installation and troubleshooting

Technologies used

Juniper Switches & APs CATO SD-WAN Microsoft Teams Telephony Pure IP Vodafone Circuit Genetec Security Microsoft 365

Impact & Outcome

Before: No IT infrastructure, lack of connectivity, no compliant security system.

After: Fully functional site with Juniper LAN, CATO WAN, Teams telephony, Vodafone internet, and GDPR-compliant Genetec cameras.

Business value: Enabled Spirol’s global expansion into Central Europe with secure, scalable, and compliant IT infrastructure — delivered on time and integrated into Spirol’s 10+ global sites.
